Pool deck

Scroll down

The Empire Hotel's rooftop Pool Deck is a one-of-a-kind.

The Empire Hotel's rooftop Pool Deck is the ultimate oasis for relaxation. The sky is the limit as you take in the scenic city skyline views while enjoying a refreshing dip in the plunge pool or you are enjoying the shade from the comfort of our private cabanas atop our boutique Manhattan hotel. The Empire Hotel's Pool Deck is exclusively for guest use and it can be reserved for private events as well. While relaxing, guests can also take advantage of the Pool Deck menu for some tasty snacks and delicious beverages.

CELEBRATION PACKAGES ON THE POOL DECK

Available May 22 - Sep 7

Bring your bikini and hold your next Bridal Shower, Bachelorette Party or Baby Shower on our Pool Deck. Beverage packages from $ 45.00 per person. For additional packages or more information please email Events@EmpireHotelNYC.com
Open seasonally Memorial Day through Labor Day
