Welcome to the Empire Hotel

Discover all that New York City has to offer.

Residing at the gateway to the Upper West Side, the Empire Hotel reflects the earth tones and modernism of our famed neighbors. The New York hotel offers sophisticated comfort and bold decor among many of their upscale amenities and superior guest services.
The Empire Hotel's coveted location puts guests within convenient proximity to some of New York's top attractions. For instance, just across the street, guests can overlook the renowned Lincoln Center, host to some of the top live performances and major events in the world all year-round. In the opposite direction, guests will find Central Park and Columbus Circle less than a few short blocks away.
Unwind at The Empire Hotel in style and comfort after a long day in the city. Each of the Upper West Side guestrooms is finely decorated, offers premium bedding and designer toiletries. In addition, the Central Park hotel also offers a popular rooftop bar and lounge, a seasonal rooftop pool, a lobby bar, and a mezzanine level of ballrooms to host your events, weddings or meetings.
Come stay at The Empire Hotel to discover all that Manhattan has to offer!
