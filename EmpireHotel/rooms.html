Deluxe King

Spread out in this oversized king-bedded guest room with beautiful Lincoln Center views

Room is approximately 330 square feet. Design is a mix of earth-tone colors and functionality featuring a flat screen TV, iHome docking station with speakers, European linens and bathrobes, spa-inspired bathroom design with a tropical rain showerhead and luxury bath amenities. Enjoy our complimentary fitness center during your stay. WiFi available.

Standard Queen

Rest and relax in this warm, quiet courtyard room

Room features one queen bed (approx. 191 square feet). Design is a mix of earth-tone colors and functionality featuring a flat screen TV, alarm clock radio, European linens and bathrobes, spa-inspired bathroom design with a tropical rain showerhead and luxury bath amenities. Enjoy our complimentary fitness center during your stay. WiFi available.

Junior suite king

Ideal for longer stays, this junior suite features city views

Room is equipped with a king bed, queen size pull-out sofa bed and 2 club chairs, approximately 350 square feet. Room design is a mix of earth-tone colors and functionality featuring a flat screen TV, iHome docking stations with speakers, European linens and bathroom design with a tropical rain showerhead and luxury bath amenities. Enjoy our complimentary fitness center during your stay. WiFi available.
