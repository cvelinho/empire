FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Does the hotel provide free Wi-Fi?

We provide complimentary Wi-Fi in the lobby and mezzanine area. To have Wi-Fi access in guest rooms you need to purchase via empire landing page; $9.95 per day/per device.

Do you have a fitness center? What kind equipments do you have? What are the hours?

We have a complimentary fitness center in the lower lobby area available for 24 hours. Fitness center is equipped with free weights stations, cardio stations (treadmill & elliptical) and multipurpose station for weight lifting.

Do you have a business center?

We have a business center located in the hotel lobby.
Fee: $5.00 for every 20 minutes.
Complimentary: printing boarding pass.

Do you offer free shuttle services from the airport?

We do not offer free shuttle services. For Town Car Assistance: please reach out to our concierge team: concierge@empirehotelnyc.com
Approximate pricing for TLC yellow taxis:
LGA: $50 (approximate price)
NWR: $100 (approximate price)
JFK: $75 (approximate price)

Do you have an on-site parking? Do you offer a valet parking? What is the price?

We do offer valet parking:
$60 (24hours, no in & out privileges)
Self Parking:
$40 (24hours, no in & our privileges)
It is located (20W 64th Street; ICON PARKING)

I booked through an online travel agent website, other than the hotel website and I need to make a change to my reservation. How do I do this?

You would need to reach out to the source directly. ie: (booking.com & expedia.com)

Who can I email to request a copy of my bill from my recent stay?

Customerservice@empirehotelnyc.com

Do you offer an in-room safe for guests?

All of our guest rooms are equipped with in-room safes for your convenience.

Is the hotel room equipped with a mini-fridge?

All of our guest rooms are equipped with fully stocked private bar (charged based on consumptions). Per request and availability; we can send up an extra fridge. To empty out fully stocked mini bar; a one-time service charge of $50 will be applied to your final bill.

Do you offer a complimentary morning coffee and tea?

We do not offer complimentary morning coffee and tea.

Do you have a restaurant on site? Room Service? Bar?

We have Ed’s Chowder located on the 2nd floor mezzanine level serving breakfast, lunch and dinner.
Room Service is available during 2 meal periods only
Breakfast: (6:30AM to 10AM)
Dinner: (6PM to 10PM)

What is the price for your breakfast?

Breakfast: Continental Cold Buffet: $12 per person (excluding tax and gratuity)
Dinner: $26 per person (excluding tax and gratuity)

Can I store my luggage with the hotel upon check out? Is there a fee?

Registered hotel guests can store luggage with us without a fee.

Is your hotel affiliated with any airlines for mileage points?

We do not have a loyalty program establishes as are an independent lifestyle hotel. However, we monitor our repeat and corporate clients where we offer complimentary upgrades and courtesy early check in / late check out (based on business demand).

How can I confirm my reservation?

Please call the hotel and press #2

How can I modify my existing reservation?

For Non Hotel Website Bookings: you would need to contact the booking source directly.
For Hotel Direct Bookings: Please call the hotel and press #2

How can I cancel my existing reservation?

For Non Hotel Website Bookings: you would need to contact the booking source directly.
For Hotel Direct Bookings: Please call the hotel and press # 1 (front desk) and/or email.
